Author Guidelines

Publication Conditions & Mechanisms

🛡️ Ethical Conditions

  • Research must be strictly original. Any plagiarism results in immediate rejection.
  • Content accuracy, empirical validity, and data reliability must be thoroughly verified.
  • Paper must not be previously published or concurrently submitted to another journal.
  • Authors must declare any potential conflicts of interest influencing research outcome.

📋 Technical Conditions

  • Must include author name, affiliation, and active email address.
  • Title written in bold (font 16). Text body font 14 (Simplified Arabic) or font 12 (Times New Roman) for English/French.
  • Margins of 1.5 cm with double line spacing. Paper length between 5,000 and 8,000 words.
  • Dual abstracts required (Arabic and foreign language English/French) of 200–250 words each.
  • Strict adherence to APA 7th Edition citation and referencing format.

⚙️ Publishing Mechanisms & Review Timeline

  • Submit Word format paper via email to gimseditors@gmail.com and gloria1academy@gmail.com.
  • Preliminary evaluation assessment response sent within 72 hours.
  • Constructive peer-review report delivered within 6 to 8 weeks.
  • Revised version submitted with a summary response report addressing reviewer notes.
  • Electronic publication upon final approval, with electronic and hard copies provided to authors.
