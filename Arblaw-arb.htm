Arbitration Curriculum

Arbitration Law Procedure Courses

Four 45-hour specialized courses mastering arbitration mechanisms, the role of arbitrators, international tribunals, and panel formations.

Course 1 — 45 Hours

Arbitration as a Process

In-depth study of arbitration agreements, jurisdiction rules, procedural framework, and initial dispute filing.

Course 2 — 45 Hours

Role of the Arbitrator

Ethical duties, impartiality, evidence examination, tribunal management, and preliminary rulings.

Course 3 — 45 Hours

International Arbitration

Cross-border conflict resolution, international conventions (New York Convention), and choice of law governing treaties.

Course 4 — 45 Hours

Arbitration Award & Panel Formation

Drafting enforceable arbitral awards, challenging decisions, enforcement mechanisms, and panel composition.
