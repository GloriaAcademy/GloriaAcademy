ABOUT US

GIMS is a scientific peer reviewed journal which is generally recognized by the Lebanese University and published in Lebanon periodically every four months. In addition, there will be special issues published exceptionally to keep abreast of the new scientific conferences. The journal was founded in 2022 by an elite of specialists in the field of integrating ICT in education. It receives research papers in three languages (Arabic, English and French). Besides, a competent and independent scientific board which includes the best reviewers and editors oversees the editing of each issue. Moreover, GIMS is licensed from the Ministry of Media and has the right to publish all its issues in the Lebanese Official Gazette.
