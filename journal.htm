About Us

Gloria for International Multidisciplinary Studies (GIMS) is a peer-reviewed scientific journal, officially recognized by the Lebanese University and published in Lebanon periodically every four months. In addition to its regular issues, GIMS occasionally publishes special issues exceptionally to keep abreast the latest developments presented in scientific conferences. The journal was founded in 2022 by an elite of specialists in the field of integrating ICT in education. It receives research papers in three languages (Arabic, English and French). Besides, a competent and independent scientific board which includes the best reviewers and editors oversees the editing of each issue ensuring the highest standards of academic quality. GIMS is licensed by the Ministry of Media and holds the right to publish all its issues in the Lebanese Official Gazette.
