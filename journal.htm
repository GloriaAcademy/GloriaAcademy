Journal Overview

Gloria for International Multidisciplinary Studies (GIMS) is a peer-reviewed scientific journal, officially recognized by the Lebanese University and published in Lebanon periodically every four months. In addition to its regular issues, GIMS occasionally publishes special issues exceptionally to keep abreast of the latest developments presented in scientific conferences.

Founded in 2022 by an elite group of specialists in ICT integration in education, GIMS receives research papers across three languages: Arabic, English, and French. An independent scientific board of top reviewers and editors oversees the peer-review process, ensuring the highest standards of academic excellence.

GIMS is licensed by the Ministry of Media and holds full authorization to publish all issues in the official gazette.
