PUBLICATION CONDITIONS PUBLICATION FEES


The researcher pays the publication fee after peer reviewing and before publication. The fee is 90$ for papers sent from inside Lebanon and 150$ for papers received from outside Lebanon. Due to the economic crisis, the fee for Lebanese students exclusively is 70$. 3-5 days to get peer review results using the fast track will cost authors 100$. Authors who would like to publish papers that exceed 8,000 words need to pay an extra amount of money, depending on the number of extra words.
