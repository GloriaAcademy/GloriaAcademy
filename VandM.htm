Vission and Mission

Editors seek to choose the best theoretical and practical scientific research and studies that conform to international scientific standards as well as those that are characterized by excellence and originality. Based on this, the publications aim to share high quality research after being peer reviewed. This is done directly or indirectly to expand the prospects, knowledge and skills to meet all the contemporary challenges by creating a database of research and studies.
