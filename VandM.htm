Vision & Mission

Our Commitment to Academic Excellence

Editors seek to choose the best theoretical and practical scientific research and studies that conform to international scientific standards as well as those that are characterized by excellence and originality.

Based on this, GIMS publications aim to share high quality research after being peer-reviewed. This is done directly or indirectly to expand prospects, knowledge, and skills to meet contemporary global challenges by creating a robust database of research and multidisciplinary studies.
